Crystal Castles

Sobre Spotify

Crystal Castles is an experimental electronic band formed in Toronto, Canada, in 2006. The group originally consisted of vocalist Alice Glass and producer/multi-instrumentalist Ethan Kath. Their unique style blends lo-fi, 8-bit (inspired by retro video game sounds), electropunk, and noise, resulting in a dark, chaotic, and sometimes melodic sound.

Important albums:

  • Crystal Castles (2008)
  • Crystal Castles (II) (2010)
  • Crystal Castles (III) (2012)
  • Amnesty (I) (2016) (featuring new vocalist Edith Frances)
